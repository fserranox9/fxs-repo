Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken pieces
  • 3 dried guajillo chiles
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp oregano, salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast chiles and soak until soft.
  2. Blend chiles, garlic, and vinegar to make adobo sauce.
  3. Marinate chicken in sauce for 2 hours.
  4. Simmer until tender — serve with rice and tortillas.
